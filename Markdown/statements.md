* [be](be.html)
* compare blocks
* copy blocks

* [copy story](copy-story.html)
* [copy stories](copy-stories.html)
* [delete story](delete-story.html)
* delete stories
* [publish story](publish-story.html)
* publish stories
* unpublish story
